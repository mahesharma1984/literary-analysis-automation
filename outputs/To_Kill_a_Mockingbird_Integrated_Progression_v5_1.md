## WEEK 1: Exposition

### Reading Assignment
- **Read:** Chapters 1-31
- **Analyze:** Chapter 1 (primary focus)
- **Covers:** climax, exposition, resolution

### Macro Concept
**Exposition:** How characters and setting are introduced

**Teaching Goal:** Understanding how exposition is built through devices

**Teaching Approach:** Exposition is BUILT through transparent narrative devices.

### Pedagogical Structure
**Scaffolding Level:** High - Teacher models everything

**Teaching Sequence:**
1. Introduce concept of literary devices as author's tools
2. Define exposition and its components
3. Practice identifying exposition devices in text together
4. Explain what each device accomplishes
5. Begin simple TVODE construction
